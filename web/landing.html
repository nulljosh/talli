Tally

Tally pulls your BC Self-Serve data into one clean dashboard. Benefits, payments, messages -- no portal maze required. Sign in to get started.

What you get

  • Payments at a glance -- See your next payment date and amount instantly. No more logging in and clicking through five screens to find a number.
  • Messages & notifications -- All your BC Self-Serve messages and notifications in one clean list. Read them without fighting the government portal UI.
  • Your credentials, secure -- Your logins are encrypted end-to-end. Tally never stores passwords in plain text or shares your data.
